    • About Us

    Recognizing social constraints during a pandemic, we aim to bridge isolated students and offer opportunities to connect with others on similar (or not so similar!) interests. Additionally, we aim to connect students to mentors in education, career fields, and everything in between.

    • Opt-in randomized meetings for low-commitment
    • Streamlined meeting scheduling for convenient planning
    • Verified Berkeley students

    Created by Connect@Cal, Berkeley's one-stop hub for resources and question answers. Whether you're an aspring baker, curious about coding, or want to find movie recs, we bet you're bound to find someone with the same interests here. Make a bio today and start chit chatting today!

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does it take to schedule a meeting?

    After finding a person you're interested in meeting, just click their bio to their calendly page and schedule a meeting in 2 minutes.

    What if the available mentors are not a great fit?

    No worries at all! We are constantly updating our database of mentors so check back in another time and we may have a mentor that fits your interests.

    What if the person never shows up to our "Chit Chat"?

    Please send a feedback form and mark that they didn't attend. After 3 missed scheduled meetings (that they confirmed to attend) they will no longer be allowed to participate in this program.
